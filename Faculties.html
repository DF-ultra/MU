üè† Main Campus (Endayesus)

The Main Campus (also known as Endayesus Campus) serves as the primary administrative and academic hub for the university. It hosts a diverse range of colleges and institutes focusing on foundational sciences, technology, and humanities.

Key Academic Units:

  • College of Natural and Computational Sciences
  • College of Law and Governance
  • College of Social Sciences and Languages
  • College of Dryland Agriculture and Natural Resources Management (Sometimes referred to as part of the Main area)
  • Ethiopian Institute of Technology - Mekelle (EiT-M) (Often at a separate but related site like Aynalem/MIT Campus, but academically grouped with Engineering/Technology)
  • Various Institutes (e.g., Pedagogical Sciences, Paleo-Environment)

üíº Business Campus (Adi Haki)

The Business Campus is dedicated to the fields of business, management, and economics. It has been a historical part of the university's development and focuses on fostering entrepreneurial and economic development skills.

Key Academic Unit:

  • College of Business and Economics (CBE), which includes departments like:
    • Management
    • Economics
    • Accounting and Finance
    • Marketing Management
    • Cooperative Programs

üè• Ayder Campus

The Ayder Campus is the site of the university's health and medical disciplines, closely integrated with the **Ayder Comprehensive Specialized Hospital**. It is central to the training of healthcare professionals and medical research in the region.

Key Academic Unit:

  • College of Health Sciences(CHS), which includes Schools of:
    • Medicine (including Ayder Referral Hospital)
    • Public Health
    • Nursing
    • Pharmacy
    • Medical Laboratory Science
    • Other Allied Health fields

üêæ Veterinary Campus

The Veterinary Campus (often dedicated to the College of Veterinary Sciences) focuses on animal health, livestock production, and veterinary public health. It plays a crucial role in supporting Ethiopia's agricultural sector.

Key Academic Unit:

  • College of Veterinary Science (CVS), with departments such as:
    • Veterinary Clinical Medicine and Epidemiology
    • Veterinary Public
