Previous Presidents Of Mekelle University


DR. Fana hagos

2021~

Prior to her presidency, she served as the President of the Tigray Regional Supreme Court for four years and six months. Academic Background: She holds a Ph.D. degree from Warwick University, UK. She has a Master's degree from the University of Free Africa. She received her bachelor's degree in law from Addis Ababa University.

prof. fetyen abay

2020-2021

She is a tenured Professor of Plant Breeding and Seed (Crop Science).She was the first female lecturer at Mekelle University when she joined in 1993. ​She is Ethiopia's first female professor of Plant Breeding and Seed (Agriculture). ​She is the country's third woman to attain the rank of full professor.

prof. kindeya G/hiwot

2013-2020

Full Professor of Forestry at Mekelle University (MU). Ph.D. from the University of Göttingen, Germany (2003). ​M.Sc. in Environmental Forestry from the University of Wales (Bangor), UK (1996).

Dr. joachim herzig

2010-2013

He holds a PhD in Microbiology and a Habilitation (DSc) in Human Physiology from the University of Heidelberg, Germany.

prof. mitku haile

2000-2010

He is universally recognized as the Founding Father and First President of Mekelle University (MU). He is a Professor of Soil Science and Sustainable Land Management.
